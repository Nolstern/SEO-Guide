What Changed in the June 2025 Update?

Google's June core update emphasized content quality and site authority even more. Pages that had thin content, outdated info, or overly SEO-optimized formatting saw drops in traffic. Google appears to be better at identifying helpful, human-centered writing ‚Äî even if it's from smaller domains.

Beginner Tips to Adapt Now

  • Refresh Your Old Content: Google wants updated, relevant pages. Don't leave your articles untouched for a year.
  • Focus on Fewer, Better Pages: It's better to have 10 strong guides than 50 shallow ones.
  • Use Clear Language: Avoid robotic phrasing. Write like you'd explain something to a friend.
  • Add Internal Links: Help users (and Google) navigate your site more easily.
  • Watch Performance Metrics: In GA4, track average engagement time, bounce rate, and user retention over time.
